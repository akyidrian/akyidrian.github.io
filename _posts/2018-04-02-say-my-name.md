**Welcome to my new blog called "Akyidrian"!** Yes, it is a rather odd name for a blog and you may even suspect it is the name of an ancient Greek hero. **A**k**y**i**d**r**i**a**n**. Is it obvious now? The emboldened letters spell out "Aydin" and the normal letter in-between spell out "Arik" in reverse. It is simply my name as an anagram! 

The **main purpose of this blog** will be document my personal projects and help organise, clarify and share my thoughts around the new technologies and topics I'm learning. Almost anything relating to programming and software engineering will be fair game. There will likely be sprinkles of computer science, data science and mathematics involved too. As a bonus, I hope to connect and learn from others like yourself during the process; so **please do comment! Criticisms are especially welcome!**

**So why such a cryptic blog name?** The simplest explanation is that I couldn't think of anything better. However, there is a point to be made with the name chosen: on first impression almost any problem can seem complicated or impossible to solve, but with some perseverance simple, ordered and explainable patterns often do result.
