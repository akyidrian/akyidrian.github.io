**Welcome to my new blog called "Akyidrian"!** Yes, it is a rather odd name for a blog, and you may even suspect it is the name of an ancient Greek hero. **A**\_k\_**y**\_i\_**d**\_r\_**i**\_a\_**n**. Is it obvious now? It's simply my name as an anagram! The emboldened letters spell out "Aydin" and the normal letters in-between spell "Arik" in reverse.

The **main purpose of this blog** will be to document my personal projects and help organise, clarify and share my thoughts around the new technologies and topics I'm learning. Almost anything relating to programming and software engineering will be fair game. There will likely be sprinkles of AI, algorithms, data science, embedded systems and mathematics involved too! As a bonus, I hope to connect and learn from others like yourself during the process; so **please do comment! Criticisms are especially welcome!**

**So why such a cryptic blog name?** The simplest explanation is I couldn't think of anything better. However, there is a point to be made with the name chosen: on first impression almost any problem can seem wildly complicated or impossible to solve, but with some perseverance often simple, ordered and explainable patterns can be found. Let's see how many patterns (or solutions) I manage to find!
